ABOUT :

This club is a part of language club in Sage university. Although this particular website contain the information related to japanese language club and other resources. The main goal of this club is to help and make the club members clear the N5 stage (Easiest stage) of JLPT (An Official Embassy run exam). This level of JLPT will provide the foundation of mastering the Japanese language. We are interested in providing the dedicated students adequate material, required information, proper guidance and teaching the concepts of language free of cost.
